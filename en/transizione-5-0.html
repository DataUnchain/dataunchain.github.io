🏛️ The National Plan

What is Transition 5.0?

Piano Transizione 5.0 is the Italian government's program, managed by MIMIT (Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy), that incentivizes companies to invest in digitalization, artificial intelligence, and energy efficiency through automatic tax credits.

Unlike traditional grants, Transition 5.0 is not competitive: there's no ranking, no scoring. If your investment meets the requirements, the tax credit is automatic. You offset it directly in your F24 tax form, reducing taxes owed to the State. In practice, the government reimburses a portion of your investment — up to 45% for small enterprises.

The program evolved from the previous Transition 4.0 Plan and incorporates 2025-2026 European priorities: technological sovereignty, AI made in Europe, reduced dependence on non-EU cloud services, and SME digitalization. DataUnchain fits perfectly into all these directives — which is why it represents a particularly strategic investment for those seeking these incentives.

📋 Process Overview

How the Tax Credit Works

Four steps to your tax rebate. No bidding, no ranking.

1

Ex-Ante Certification

Before the investment, an accredited certifier (registered engineer or surveyor) verifies that the asset falls within eligible categories and that the investment will produce documentable energy savings.

Typical cost: €500-€2,000 · Timeline: 1-2 weeks

2

Purchase & Installation

You purchase the DataUnchain appliance and install it at your company. Payment must be traceable (bank transfer) and the invoice must include the correct regulatory references (Law 232/2016, Piano Transizione 5.0).

DataUnchain: complete setup in 2-4 hours · Operational same day

3

Ex-Post Certification

After installation, the same certifier verifies that the asset has been effectively interconnected with company systems and that the projected energy savings have been achieved. For investments over €300,000, a sworn expert report is required.

Typical cost: €500-€2,000 · Timeline: 1-2 weeks

4

F24 Tax Offset

The tax credit is used directly as an offset in the F24 tax form, reducing taxes and contributions owed. It's not a refund: it's a direct reduction of taxes you owe. Immediate, automatic, no waiting.

Offsettable starting from the fiscal year following the investment

📝 Requirements Checklist

What You Need to Access the Credit

Be an Italian enterprise

Any legal form (SRL, SPA, sole proprietorship, partnerships). Italian tax residency. No size limit: applies to micro, small, medium, and large enterprises.

Ex-ante certification

A qualified technician certifies that the asset falls within eligible categories before purchase. For DataUnchain (4.0 capital asset with AI software), this is a standard procedure.

Interconnection with company systems

The asset must be interconnected with the company's management system (ERP, accounting, warehouse). DataUnchain natively integrates with 18 Italian and international ERPs — the requirement is met by the standard installation.

Ex-post certification

After installation, effective interconnection and energy savings are verified. For investments over €300,000, a sworn expert report is required. For DataUnchain bundles (€2,100-€8,100), a simple attestation suffices.

Traceable payment

Bank transfer with a description referencing the relevant regulations. No cash, no checks. The purchase invoice must include references to Law 232/2016 and Piano Transizione 5.0.

❓ FAQ

Transition 5.0 + DataUnchain

Does DataUnchain really qualify for Transition 5.0?
Yes. DataUnchain consists of dedicated hardware (tangible capital asset — Annex A) and AI software (intangible capital asset — Annex B). Both fall within categories specified by Law 232/2016 updated by Piano Transizione 5.0. The interconnection requirement is natively met by the 18 integrated ERP connectors. We recommend verifying with your accountant for your specific situation.
How much does ex-ante/ex-post certification cost?
For investments of DataUnchain's size (€2,100-€8,100), certification is relatively simple and inexpensive. A registered engineer or surveyor typically charges €500-€2,000 for both certifications. The certification cost itself is eligible for the credit. No sworn expert report is required for investments under €300,000 — a simple attestation suffices.
Is the annual software license eligible?
The AI software falls under Annex B intangible assets (software, systems and system integration, platforms and applications). The annual license can be eligible as a 4.0 intangible asset fee. For details on fee vs. purchase deductibility, consult your accountant — rules vary based on the contractual form.
Can I stack Transition 5.0 with other incentives?
Generally, the Transition 5.0 tax credit is stackable with other incentives that don't cover the same expenses. For example, you could use a Chamber of Commerce digitalization voucher for one portion and the tax credit for the rest. Stacking rules change periodically — always verify with your advisor for current regulations at the time of investment.
Does DataUnchain help with credit documentation?
Yes. We provide all technical documentation needed for certification: hardware specifications, software description, interconnection protocol, energy and time savings metrics. Your expert or consultant will have everything needed to prepare both ex-ante and ex-post certifications without issues. We guide you through the process from start to finish.
