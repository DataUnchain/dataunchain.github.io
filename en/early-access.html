❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? That's normal.

Do I need a powerful GPU?
For maximum speed (~30 seconds/document) you need an NVIDIA GPU with at least 16GB VRAM — like an RTX 3090 or 4090. But DataUnchain also works in CPU mode on a regular PC: it's slower (~3-5 minutes/document) but perfectly functional for low volumes. During the Early Adopter program we help you evaluate the best hardware for your volume.
Does it really work without internet?
Yes. Once installed, DataUnchain doesn't need the internet to function. The AI model runs entirely locally. The only time a connection is needed is for the initial installation (downloading the software and the model). After that, it can work in completely air-gapped environments.
What happens after the 6 free months?
You decide. If the product satisfied you, you can subscribe to an annual license at a discounted rate (locked forever as an early adopter). If you don't want to continue, we uninstall everything at no cost and with no strings attached. No contract lock-in, no exit penalty. The data you processed stays yours — it was always on your machine.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. DataUnchain works 100% locally: your documents are never sent to the internet, to a cloud, or to any external server. The AI model runs on your physical machine, in your network. No cloud sub-processor, no remote logs, no telemetry. Your data is yours, stays yours, and is protected by your firewall and your security policies — not by ours.
How long does installation take?
The technical installation takes about 2 hours — Docker Compose, configuration, and model download. The specific configuration for your ERP and your suppliers takes another 2-4 hours spread over the first week. In total, from first call to full operation, about 2-3 weeks.
Do I need to provide test documents?
Yes, we ask for a sample of 20-50 documents (the ones you handle most frequently) to calibrate the system for your specific layouts. These documents are processed directly on your machine during installation — we don't receive them, we don't see them. They stay where they've always been: in your office.
Why is it free? What's the catch?
No catch. DataUnchain is a new product — working and tested, but new to the market. To grow we need two things: real feedback from real businesses, and credible testimonials to prove the product works. Those two things are worth far more to us than the $5,000 we're giving away. It's an investment we're making on trust: if the product works as we believe, we gain a satisfied long-term customer. If it doesn't, we'd rather know now than later.
