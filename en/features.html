Double-Check

Process the same document with two different AI models. If the results diverge, it flags for human review.

Every field receives a confidence percentage. Low confidence fields are highlighted for verification.

The AI processes 100 documents in 2 minutes. Humans verify only the 3-4 flagged ones. 90% time saved, 0% errors.
